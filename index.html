The Solution

A device that evolves.
Software. Hardware. Perpetually.

🧠

AI-Powered OS Evolution

Yphone Core continuously monitors performance, predicts bottlenecks, and deploys system optimizations — without a single user interaction. It gets smarter every day.

🔩

Modular Hardware Architecture

CPU, GPU, memory, sensors — every component is a swappable module. Yphone identifies the bottleneck and replaces only that module. Not the whole device.

🖨️

AI-Designed + 3D-Printed Parts

When a new module is needed, Yphone designs it, sends it to a distributed 3D-print network, and delivers it to your door. Plug-and-play in seconds.

♻️

100% Circular Regeneration

Every replaced module is collected, refurbished by Yphone, and redeployed into another device. Nothing becomes waste. Everything becomes a resource.

🔗

Blockchain Component Provenance

Each hardware module carries an on-chain record — from fabrication to every refurbishment. Full traceability. Trust in every component.

🌍

Zero E-Waste by 2050

Our mission: eliminate consumer electronics waste entirely. Every chip, every sensor, every gram of rare earth — in permanent, productive use.
